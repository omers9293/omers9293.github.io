SEP11 Freedom Project

The “Freedom Project” for SEP11 revolves around creating an application that uses Javascript to perform one/multiple functions with a tool that you learned on your own. The game that I've created helps tackle America's biggest weakness that is not addressed in the classrooms which is students not knowing enough geography. Research has shown that studying geography helps students develop a sense of place and space, and spatial awareness which helps them navigate the world and their place within it.

The geography game that I have created enables the user to practice their ability to recall countries on the map as much as they would like. I have included markers so the user is aware of all the countries they entered and will not have the ability to zoom in because that defeats the entire purpose of the game.
