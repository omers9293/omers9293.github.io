SEP10 Freedom Project

The “Freedom Project” for SEP10 is all about creating an informative website based on a non-tech topic using a tool that you learned on your own. This informative website includes the past history of technology being used in the field, current technology used, and future possibilities that can be created in the future. The purpose of this blog is to provide people with my thought process and the obstacles that I have faced to complete this project. The information provided in this blog can give a different perspective on the website/ presentation making this a behind-the-scenes.

For my project, I am making a website about Medical Practice using HTML, CSS, and Github. This blog will walk through my process of independently studying Jekyll and making the website. Creating this website was without a doubt one of my greatest accomplishments throughout high school so far which didn't happen overnight. This blog certifies that it took an entire school year of learning, questioning, homework, and many more to come to this point where I can create a website, after having zero prior experience in this field.
